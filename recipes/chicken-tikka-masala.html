Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients required:

  • 500 grams boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons tikka masala spice mix
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, tikka masala spice mix, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Add chicken pieces to the yogurt mixture and marinate for at least 30 minutes (overnight is best).
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until they turn translucent.
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook until it's no longer pink, stirring occasionally.
  5. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Stir in the heavy cream and simmer for an additional 5 minutes
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and serve with rice or naan.
